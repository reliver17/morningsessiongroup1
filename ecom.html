ABOUT NATURE HILLS

Nature Hills was originally founded in 2004 as a conifer and deciduous tree farm. Nature Hills started as a local tree farm serving a limited geographic area. Our company has evolved and responded to our customers' demand of quality farm tree products. We continue to improve our on-line capabilities and expand our product offerings. We have extended our offerings outside the tree and shrub products, to include perennials, rose bushes and other plants, as well as flower bulbs, seeds and a complete garden center. We plan to continue to expand products to meet the needs of our customers.
Our growth over the years has remained tied to the Nature Hills Farm mission: Our mission is to continually improve all aspects of our business in order to exceed our nationwide customers' expectations. Nature Hills farm strives to offer one of the largest selections of high quality live plants and related products, complete with extensive plant information, while maintaining an exceptional level of customer service. We will not sacrifice the personal attention we give our customers for our expanded growth.
